Toronto Gaymers is Toronto’s original QUILTBAG social group for geeks and gamers of all stripes. The group started in 2007 from GayGamer.net’s discussion forums as a semi-annual gathering in downtown Toronto. The group has since evolved into a safe space for QUILTBAG people and their allies to express their passion for geek and gaming culture, including board games, trading card games, PC and console games, cosplay, anime and more. Through our monthly social gaming events, we support local businesses and strengthen our community.
